Online & Telehealth Therapy

About Telehealth

Recovery Without Barriers

Golden State Rehab's telehealth IOP delivers the same evidence-based treatment as our in-person program — through a secure, HIPAA-compliant video platform. If you live outside of Los Angeles, have transportation challenges, or simply feel more comfortable at home, telehealth brings quality care directly to you.

You'll attend live group therapy sessions, meet individually with your therapist, consult with our psychiatrist, and participate in the full spectrum of our IOP curriculum — all from your computer, tablet, or phone.

Get Started
How It Works

Getting Started with Telehealth

1

Call or Apply Online

Reach our admissions team by phone or complete a brief online assessment to begin the intake process.

2

Insurance Verified

We verify your coverage and explain any costs before your first session — completely free and confidential.

3

Platform Setup

We'll send you a simple link to our secure telehealth platform. No downloads needed — works in any browser.

4

Orientation Session

Meet your primary therapist and the group in a virtual orientation that walks you through expectations and scheduling.

5

Begin Treatment

Join your scheduled sessions — groups, individual therapy, and psychiatric check-ins — from wherever you are.

6

Aftercare Planning

Your team coordinates your transition to ongoing outpatient care, local resources, and alumni support at graduation.

Is Telehealth Right For You?

Who Benefits from Online IOP

Our telehealth program serves clients throughout California who can't access our Los Angeles facility or prefer the flexibility of remote treatment.

  • Clients outside the Los Angeles metro area
  • Those with transportation barriers
  • Parents or caregivers with at-home responsibilities
  • Professionals seeking discreet, flexible scheduling
  • Clients who feel more comfortable in their own environment

About IOP

What is an Intensive Outpatient Program?

An Intensive Outpatient Program (IOP) provides structured clinical treatment that allows clients to live at home or in supportive housing while maintaining work, school, or family responsibilities. It is a step below PHP in intensity, but delivers the same evidence-based therapeutic depth.

Golden State Rehab's IOP meets 3–5 days per week for 3-hour sessions, totaling 9–15 hours of treatment per week. We offer morning and evening scheduling options so recovery fits your life — not the other way around.

What Our IOP Covers

Group Therapy

Daily therapeutic groups covering CBT, DBT, relapse prevention, trauma processing, and interpersonal skills.

Individual Therapy

Weekly one-on-one sessions with your primary therapist to deepen your personal recovery work.

Psychiatric Services

Access to our board-certified psychiatrist for medication evaluation, management, and MAT support.

Family Therapy

Sessions designed to repair relationships, educate loved ones, and strengthen the support system around you.

Psychoeducation

Education on addiction, mental health, neuroscience of recovery, and relapse prevention planning.

Holistic Wellness

Mindfulness, breathwork, yoga, and movement therapy integrated into the weekly schedule.

Is IOP Right For You?

Who Benefits Most from IOP

IOP is ideal for clients who have established a baseline of stability — either through completing PHP or entering treatment with a stable support system and living environment.

  • Clients transitioning from PHP ready for more independence
  • Professionals who need to maintain work or school
  • Parents or caregivers with significant home responsibilities
  • Individuals with a stable home or sober living situation
  • Those with mild to moderate addiction or mental health symptoms

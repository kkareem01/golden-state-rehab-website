About Medication Management

Psychiatric Care as Part of Integrated Treatment

At Golden State Rehab, medication management is not an afterthought — it's a fully integrated component of our clinical programming. Our board-certified psychiatrist works collaboratively with your primary therapist to ensure your treatment plan addresses both biological and psychological dimensions of your condition.

Whether you need medication-assisted treatment (MAT) for opioid or alcohol use disorder, psychiatric medication for depression, anxiety, or PTSD, or simply a thoughtful assessment of your current medications, our psychiatrist provides expert, compassionate care as part of your program.

About MAT

Medication-Assisted Treatment (MAT)

MAT combines FDA-approved medications with counseling and behavioral therapies to treat substance use disorders. It is one of the most evidence-based approaches to opioid and alcohol addiction treatment available.

Research shows MAT improves client survival, increases treatment retention, decreases illicit drug use, and improves birth outcomes for pregnant women with substance use disorder. At Golden State Rehab, we view MAT as a valuable tool — not a crutch — and integrate it seamlessly into your overall recovery plan.

  • Buprenorphine (Suboxone) for opioid use disorder
  • Naltrexone (Vivitrol) for alcohol and opioid use disorder
  • Acamprosate for alcohol use disorder
  • All medications managed by board-certified psychiatrist

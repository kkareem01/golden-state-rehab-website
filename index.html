Process

Your path to treatment

Getting started is straightforward. We handle the details so you can focus on recovery.

01

First Contact

You call or fill out our intake form. Our team responds within hours to answer questions and schedule your assessment.

02

Clinical Assessment

A clinical interview with our intake specialist. We listen to your story, understand your needs, and discuss treatment options.

03

Psychiatric Review

Psychiatric evaluation and medical review. We check your insurance and finalize your treatment plan with the clinical team.

04

Your First Session

Your first treatment session. You meet your therapist, join group therapy, and begin the work of recovery in a supportive environment.

05

Treatment Momentum

You're in your program, building relationships with peers, and developing skills that stick. Treatment gains real momentum.

06

Continued Care

Continued care and alumni support. We stay with you through transitions and celebrate your progress every step of the way.

Our Approach

Evidence-Based, Individualized, and Compassionate

Every person who walks through our doors has a unique story — a unique combination of genetics, history, trauma, and circumstances that shaped their relationship with substances or mental health challenges. Our treatment plans reflect that reality.

We use therapies supported by decades of research: Cognitive Behavioral Therapy, EMDR, Dialectical Behavior Therapy, Motivational Interviewing, and Medication-Assisted Treatment — customized to each individual, not applied uniformly.

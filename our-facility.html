A Space for Healing

Our Facility

A calm, private setting in West Los Angeles — designed to feel like a home away from home, not a hospital.

The Environment

Designed for healing, not clinical sterility

Recovery requires more than medication and therapy — it requires an environment that feels safe, calm, and genuinely supportive. From the moment you walk through our doors, our facility was intentionally designed to put you at ease.

We chose West Los Angeles for its quiet, residential character — away from the chaos that can destabilize early recovery. Every space in our facility, from private therapy rooms to communal lounges, was selected and arranged to support the hard, meaningful work of getting well.

Our Spaces

Every room serves a purpose in your recovery

From the privacy of individual therapy to the communal warmth of group sessions, every space at Golden State Rehab is built to support a different dimension of your healing.

Private Therapy Rooms

Confidential, soundproofed spaces where you can speak openly with your therapist. Designed for comfort and trust — because honest conversation is where healing begins.

Group Therapy Suite

A welcoming, circular space designed to foster genuine connection. Our group rooms hold 6–10 participants in a setting that feels safe, warm, and supportive — not clinical.

Lounge & Common Area

A comfortable, living-room-style space where clients relax, connect, and simply breathe. Sometimes the informal moments between sessions are where real healing happens.

Outdoor Wellness Garden

A private, landscaped outdoor space for fresh air, reflection, and light movement. Access to nature — even in an urban setting — plays a measurable role in recovery outcomes.

Meditation & Yoga Space

A dedicated room for mindfulness, breathwork, and gentle movement. Our holistic therapy sessions — from yoga to meditation — take place here, free from distraction.

Relaxation Lounge

A quiet space to decompress between sessions — with soft lighting, comfortable seating, and a break from the intensity of clinical work. Rest is part of recovery too.

Design Philosophy

Every detail was a deliberate choice

We believe the physical environment plays a direct role in recovery. Research consistently shows that thoughtfully designed spaces reduce anxiety, improve treatment engagement, and support long-term outcomes.

  • Natural light throughout all therapy and common spaces
  • Private spaces for individual reflection and therapy
  • Communal areas that foster peer connection and support
  • Evidence-based spatial design principles applied throughout
  • Noise-reduced private rooms for focus and confidentiality
  • Tranquil outdoor areas accessible during program hours
Location

Quietly located in West Los Angeles

Our facility sits in a calm, residential neighborhood in West Los Angeles — far from the noise and triggers of high-traffic areas, but easily accessible from across the city and surrounding communities.

We've intentionally kept our location private and discreet. Our clients deserve to come and go without unwanted attention. Contact our admissions team for our exact address after completing your intake consultation.

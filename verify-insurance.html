How Insurance Works for Treatment

We work with most major insurance plans and our team handles everything on your behalf.

We work with most major insurance providers including Anthem Blue Cross, Aetna, Cigna, Blue Shield of California, United Healthcare, Magellan, Optum, Prime Health, TRICARE, and many others. Under the Mental Health Parity and Addiction Equity Act, most plans are required to cover behavioral health treatment.

Coverage varies by plan, but most insurance policies cover a significant portion of PHP and IOP treatment, including individual therapy, group therapy, psychiatric services, and medication management. Your out-of-pocket costs depend on your deductible, co-pay, and co-insurance amounts. Our team will give you a clear breakdown before you begin.

Our admissions team typically completes insurance verification within 1 hour during business hours. Once we verify your benefits, we'll call you to explain exactly what your plan covers and any expected costs — so you can make an informed decision with complete clarity.

If your insurance doesn't cover the full cost of treatment, we offer private pay options and flexible payment arrangements. We believe cost should never be the barrier to care. Our admissions team will work with you to find a solution that allows you to access the treatment you need.
